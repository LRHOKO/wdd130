Dry Oar Rafting Site Plan

Landon Roberts

Overview

Purpose: Provide details to customers regarding trip types, pricing, safety, and equipment. Include reviews, a preview of the experience, as well as the company's unique value. Include easy ways to allow customers to book a session either through the site or providing contact information.

Audience: Who are the target customers? Families would be the primary target. What do they want? A safe and enjoyable experience that allows for all to participate. What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? Questions regarding openings, price, age requirements, and safety measures.
